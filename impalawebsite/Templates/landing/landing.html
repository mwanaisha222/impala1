Who We Are

Impala Healthtech Research Limited

Founded with a sole purpose of building a strong evidence base for internally and externally designed health technology solutions using strong research methodologies, as a means of achieving sustainably impactful health technology interventions.

We are a team of 8 multidisciplinary, and passionate young professionals supported by a team of highly experienced non executive directors.

What We Do

We conduct both explorative and evaluative research, in addition to technology and business development

Research and Development

We conduct explorative research to establish the most pressing health challenges, and customize technology based solutions for them. We do routine, and on demand research in health systems, health service access and delivery, health economics and pharmaceutics.

Impact Evaluation

We use strong research methodologies to conduct independent impact evaluation of internally and externally developed health technology solutions, to prepare them for scaled implementation.

Business Development

We conduct market research and analysis, and use the scientific evidence generated to develop and test business and revenue models for health technology solutions with high scaled impact potential.

DIGAMS

DIGAMS is a digital, antimicrobial stewardship platform integrating a microbiology lab marketplace, and real-time A.I driven empirical antimicrobial treatment decision support to clinicians.

Patients needing pathogen confirmatory, and susceptibility tests can get their samples taken, and recorded at local laboratories or clinics, and tested at the few microbiology labs, all through DIGAMS. The results are also used to update an AI model that guides empirical antimicrobial treatment.

Underequipped facilities can conduct microbiology confirmatory or susceptibility tests from the few microbiology labs, to optimally use antimicrobial drugs.
